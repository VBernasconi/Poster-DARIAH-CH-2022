La main baladeuse

The use of computational methods within the field of art history is usually restricted to digitization and archiving tools, or more complex computational methods for statistical analysis of corpuses and patterns detection. With the project La main baladeuse, the potential of the artistic exploration is further considered. Built for the online exhibition Visual Contagions of the Jeu de Paume Museum, La main baladeuse offers to users a direct interaction with past painted and drawn hands gathered from the internet. The artwork further develop the concept of embodied knowledge and allows user to consider their own gestures as a constitutive part of an artwork shaped by many time and cultural dimensions. The contemporary hand is placed at the center of the composition, offering the perspective of an heritage of hand poses and directly question the sense of personnal gestural expressions in relation to our visual culture.
