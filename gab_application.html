Gestures for Artwork Browsing (GAB) application

At an early stage in the project came the need to search for specific items within the collection of hands. However, traditional browsing tools usually require a word input, which implies annotations of images, called metadata, and some knowledge of the content of the collection. Through a series of discussions with the DVS group, the idea of a browsing tool through gestures is born. The benefits from this approach are multiple, from which: the implication of the user's personnal gestural knowledge, the possibility to put past gestures in a contemporary perspective, and the possibility to make discoveries through unexpected gestures.
